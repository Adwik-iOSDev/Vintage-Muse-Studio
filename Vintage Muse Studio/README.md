
                                            


                                            🖤 Vintage Muse Studio



> Elevating fashion retail through technology.

Vintage Muse Studio is a premium store management platform designed for modern fashion retailers who value elegance, efficiency, and data-driven decision making.

Built with a luxury-first mindset, the platform combines inventory management, order processing, customer insights, and business analytics into a seamless mobile experience.

---

## ✨ Vision

To create the operating system for next-generation fashion retail.

Vintage Muse Studio empowers store owners with the tools they need to manage products, monitor performance, and deliver exceptional customer experiences—all from a beautifully crafted interface.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 📦 Product Management

* Create and manage product catalogs
* Organize collections and categories
* Track sizes, colors, and variants

### 📊 Inventory Intelligence

* Real-time stock monitoring
* Low-stock alerts
* Inventory movement tracking

### 🛍️ Order Operations

* Order lifecycle management
* Processing and fulfillment tracking
* Return and exchange handling

### 👥 Customer Management

* Customer profiles
* Purchase history
* Loyalty and engagement insights

### 📈 Business Analytics

* Sales performance dashboards
* Revenue tracking
* Best-selling product insights
* Growth metrics
